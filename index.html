About Me

Academic History

I received my B.S. degree from Sebha University, Sebha, Libya, in 1999. Then I received both M.Sc. and Ph.D. from the Department of Computer Science and Software Engineering of Concordia University, Montreal, Canada in 2009 and 2015, respectively. Also, in December 2015 I achieved the Diploma in Computer Networking from Champlain College, Saint-Lambert, Canada along-side full-time workload of my PhD. In June 2016, I joined the Department of Computer Science, Faculty of Technical Sciences, Sebha, Libya. My research topic of interests: First, stability, power consumption, or quality of service in Mobile Ad hoc Networks (MANETs). Second, efficient deployment of sensor networks such as design topology, routing, stability, security, or quality of service in Software-Defined Networking (SDN). Third, study the Quality of Service (QoS) aware reliable data delivery in Underwater Wireless Sensor Networks (UWSNs).
